ML Engineering & UI/UX Designer

Welcome to my GitHub profile! I'm Vinochan, a passionate and driven individual with a strong background in Computer Science, particularly in the fields of AI/ML and UI/UX design. As an undergraduate student majoring in Computer Science, I've dedicated myself to exploring the frontiers of technology and leveraging my skills to tackle real-world challenges. 

Throughout my academic journey, I've had the opportunity to gain hands-on experience through internships and projects that have enriched my understanding and proficiency in various areas. My internship at Hyundai provided me with valuable insights into UI/UX design, where I had the privilege of refining my skills and contributing to user-centric design principles. Additionally, my role as Design Lead at C2C SVCE allowed me to lead a team in developing innovative solutions, honing my leadership and project management abilities. 

My passion for technology extends beyond design to encompass artificial intelligence and machine learning. I've completed numerous projects in these areas, applying advanced algorithms to solve real-world problems. One of my notable projects, 'Revelato', is a dehaze and desmoking system that utilizes CCTV to enhance visibility during emergencies and detects exit signs in buildings for faster evacuations. Furthermore, my academic pursuits have led me to publish papers and contribute to the academic community, showcasing my commitment to research and knowledge dissemination. 

On GitHub, you'll find a collection of my projects, ranging from AI applications and machine learning models to UI/UX design prototypes. I believe in the power of collaboration and open-source contributions, and I'm excited about the prospect of sharing my work with the GitHub community and collaborating on innovative projects together. 

Thank you for visiting my GitHub profile. I invite you to explore my projects and connect with me to discuss potential collaborations or opportunities. Let's innovate and build a brighter future together!
